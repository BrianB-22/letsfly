✈ About SimLetsFly

SimLetsFly started as a personal tool to break out of flying the same old routes — a habit that's easy to fall into going all the way back to FSX. With the incredible scenery in today's simulators, there's a whole world worth exploring. This app makes it easy to discover flights you'd never think to plan yourself.

How It Works

Set your search parameters and hit Let's Fly. SimLetsFly will find a random departure and arrival airport that match your criteria. Not happy with the result? Hit it again for a different pairing, or lock in your departure and randomize only the arrival.

SimLetsFly is not a full flight planner — once you have your airports, use MSFS 2020 or your preferred tools to set up the actual flight (VFR/IFR, SID/STAR, etc). Think of it as your destination picker. All airports are compatible with MSFS 2020.

Search Parameters
Location
Continent, Country, Region, or fully Random
Range
Min and Max distance in nautical miles
Airport Size
Small, Medium, Large, and more
Runway Surface
Concrete, Grass, Water, and more
Runway Length
Min and Max length in feet
Fuel
AvGas and/or Jet-A availability
Nav Aids
Filter by ALS, VASI, and/or ILS availability
Saving & Sharing

Create a free account to save flights to your profile and share them with other pilots via a unique link. Saved flights can be reloaded at any time.
